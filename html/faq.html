FAQ

Is there a dress code?

Please wear formal attire and refrain from wearing white.

Where can we park?

There is a parking lot directly outside the church at 259 Concord St, Framingham, MA 01702 with overflow parking in the back

Can we bring the kids?

Yes! All children welcome

Can I bring a date?

Yes! You can list someone as your plus one if they do not already have their own invitation

Is there assigned seating?

No! Please sit wherever you want
